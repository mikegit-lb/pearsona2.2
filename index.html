    4Hard Final-Exam Gap-Fill Practice

    Complete the sentences. These questions focus on common A2/B1 final-exam traps.

    1. She usually _____ her phone before breakfast, but today she is studying.
    Focus: Present Simple routine
    2. There aren't _____ chairs in the classroom, so some students are standing.
    Focus: there are + any
    3. This soup has _____ salt. I can't eat it.
    Focus: uncountable quantifier
    4. We have only _____ eggs, so let's make a small omelette.
    Focus: countable quantifier
    5. My jacket doesn't _____ these trousers. The colours are very different.
    Focus: clothes vocabulary
    6. This bag isn't big _____ for all my books.
    Focus: too/enough
    7. You _____ use your phone during the final exam. It is against the rules.
    Focus: rules/prohibition
    8. I have already _____ the tickets for the school trip.
    Focus: travel expression
    9. Have you ever _____ a wild animal in nature?
    Focus: Present Perfect
    10. We are _____ to visit the science museum next Saturday.
    Focus: future plan
    11. He speaks very _____ because he practises every day.
    Focus: adverb of manner
    12. You should _____ notes during the lesson, not only listen.
    Focus: education collocation
    13. Please _____ the lights when you leave the room.
    Focus: phrasal verb
    14. I need to _____ for my keys. I can't find them anywhere.
    Focus: look for
    15. The hotel is _____ the bank and the cinema.
    Focus: preposition of place
    16. Our team made good _____ this month.
    Focus: make progress
    17. The plane will _____ off at 8:30.
    Focus: travel phrasal verb
    18. Don't _____ personal information with strangers online.
    Focus: internet safety
    19. The river is polluted, so people should _____ it.
    Focus: environment
    20. Mia is very _____. She always asks interesting questions.
    Focus: personality adjective
    Answer Key
    1. checks — Present Simple routine
    2. any — there are + any
    3. too much — uncountable quantifier
    4. a few — countable quantifier
    5. match — clothes vocabulary
    6. enough — too/enough
    7. mustn't — rules/prohibition
    8. booked — travel expression
    9. seen — Present Perfect
    10. going — future plan
    11. well — adverb of manner
    12. take — education collocation
    13. turn off — phrasal verb
    14. look — look for
    15. between — preposition of place
    16. progress — make progress
    17. take — travel phrasal verb
    18. share — internet safety
    19. protect — environment
    20. curious — personality adjective

    6Writing Tasks

    Writing Task 1

    Write 120–150 words about a school project. Use Past Simple, Present Perfect, one comparison, and five topic words.

    Writing Task 2

    Write an email to a friend about your weekend plans in the city. Use going to, Present Continuous for future, and travel expressions.

    Writing Task 3

    Write a paragraph comparing your home with your ideal home. Use furniture, house adjectives, there is/are, and comparatives.

    Writing Task 4

    Write exam advice for a younger student. Use should, must/mustn't, have to/don't have to, and life skills vocabulary.
